About Our Event

inCIToS 2K18 is a techincal festival conducted by Department of Computer Science and Information Technology, held at College of Engineering, Thalassery. It provides students various opportunities to herald their technical prowess and quiz their aptitude on coding, designing and other ambidextrous domain concepts. This is an open platform where Academics meets Innovation.
