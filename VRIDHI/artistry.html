Bring out the little civil engineer in you by building a tower!!! Coet thalassery presents Artistry- built a tower with materials like tape, straws..etc and stand to win big. So boys and girls, make a mess and wake that builder inside you and just show off your Lego skills. Come out with the best tower within the given time limit. It might win you something big.

• The building should be a tower made of minimum number of straws and thread.
• There should be 3 participants in a group.
• The base dimension of the tower should be within the diameter of 30cm.
• The height of the tower should range from 50-75cm.
• The materials provided are straw,thread,glue,scale,scissors and sellotape.
• The time allotted is 45 minutes.
• The best model will be chosen based on aesthetic view and maximum strength it can withstand without failure with less number of straws used
• Participation certificates will be provided

Prize Money depends on percentage of completion
For registration, contact event manager/cordinator.
