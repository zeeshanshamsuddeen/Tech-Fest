VRIDHI is the technical fest organised by the Civil Engineering Association of College of Engineering,Thalassery in association with ICI Students Chapter. Standing true to it's name, the inspiration behind VRIDHI is to encourage the idea of progress in the field of Engineering. Aimed at inspiring innovation,technical interest among students and awareness among the public. The event blends a plethora of technical events,lectures and a social initiative along with variety of workshops and exhibitions related to the field of Civil Engineering.

It is a platform which brings out many hidden talents to showcase. This technical fest consists of events,such as Paper Presentation, Technical Quiz, Survey Treasure Hunt and much more. It is going to be conducted on 26 and 27 February 2018. VRIDHI, is a novel attempt to bestow an oppurtunity to young engineers of tomorrow to exhibit their talents as well as to keep themselves abreast of the rapidly improvising technological world and also kindles the knowledge of young technocrats.
